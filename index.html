Will A Tactical Vote Work?

If Labour, Lib Dem and Green supporters vote tactically, could they beat the Tories?

The following simulator looks at the 2015 election results and re-allocates the Labour, Lib Dem and Green votes based on tactical voting.
It uses the tactical voting recommendations from the site www.tactical2017.com
Note that the tactical recommendations can be for parties other than the 3 mentioned above e.g. SNP and therefore such parties can receive the tactical votes. However, this simulation assumes that other party supporters will not participate in the tactical vote themselves, hence their votes are not re-allocated.

Simulation Parameters

Enter the percentage of Labour, Lib Dem and Green supporters who will vote tactically (Be realistic)

Choose a pollster. The most recent poll from the selected pollster will be used
